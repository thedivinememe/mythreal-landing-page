What Makes MythReal Different

No Classes. No Limits.

Forget picking a class. MythReal uses 20 Tech Trees — from Martial Arts to Summoning to Blood Magic — letting you build exactly the character you imagine. Mix a fire mage with a dual-wielding rogue. Combine hunting skills with arcane mastery. Your build, your rules.

Action Points Change Everything

Every turn you get 5 Action Points to spend however you want — move, attack, cast, or save them for next round. No more "I attack and end my turn." Every decision matters. Bank AP for a devastating combo, or spread your actions across movement and multiple strikes.

A Multiverse of Stories

Dragons soar through futuristic cities. Wizards and AI crack ancient mysteries together. MythReal's Mythoverse is a multiverse where mythology, sci-fi, horror, and fantasy collide. Five known worlds. Infinite adventures.

The World of Raefund

For thousands of years, Dragonkind ruled from above. The Elder Dragons led their clans with iron will and ancient magic. But when the Elders began to die, the oppressed Unter races — humans, elves, orcs — seized their chance. Now the continent of Elderlind stands divided: the New Unter Alliance in the West, the remnants of Dragonkind in the East, and a mystery that could unite or destroy them all.
